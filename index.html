Machine Catalogue

AMW Lab is equipped with heavy industrial standard machinery to aid you in your quest of manufacturing innovation. Students are able to book machines, only after they have completed the training on machine operation, and have gone over Standard Operation Protocols.

Epilog 36EXT

Laser cutter

The Epilog laser is a laser cutting and engraving machine. It can cut and engrave a wide range of materials (check our material reference on top of the printer). It also has a very powerful raster mode for engraving and etching designs. Any vector design in a PDF file format can be cut out by the machine, as well as photographic or bitmap images with varied results.
